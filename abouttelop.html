Everlasting Love Church Located in the heart of the city, Everlasting Love Church is a beacon of hope and spiritual guidance for the community. With its warm and welcoming atmosphere, the church has become a sanctuary for people from all walks of life. History Everlasting Love Church was founded in 1995 by Pastor John and his wife, Mary. With a vision to spread the message of God's love and redemption, they started the church with a small group of believers. Over the years, the church has grown exponentially, and today it is one of the largest and most vibrant churches in the city. Mission The mission of Everlasting Love Church is to share the love of God with the world and to help people develop a personal relationship with Jesus Christ. The church is committed to spreading the message of hope, redemption, and forgiveness, and to providing a supportive community for people to grow in their faith. Ministries Everlasting Love Church has a wide range of ministries that cater to different age groups and interests. Some of the ministries include: - Children's Ministry: Provides a safe and nurturing environment for children to learn about God's love. - Youth Ministry: Offers programs and activities for teenagers to grow in their faith and develop leadership skills. - Worship Ministry: Leads the congregation in worship and praise through music, dance, and other forms of expression. - Outreach Ministry: Reaches out to the community through evangelism, food drives, and other forms of service. Services Everlasting Love Church holds several services throughout the week, including: - Sunday Morning Service: A dynamic and spirit-filled service with worship, preaching, and communion. - Sunday Evening Service: A more intimate and reflective service with worship, prayer, and Bible study. - Midweek Service: A service held on Wednesdays, featuring worship, preaching, and prayer. Pastoral Team The pastoral team at Everlasting Love Church is dedicated to serving the congregation and providing spiritual guidance. The team includes: - Pastor John: Senior Pastor and founder of the church. - Pastor Mary: Co-Pastor and wife of Pastor John. - Pastor James: Associate Pastor and leader of the Youth Ministry. - Pastor Sarah: Associate Pastor and leader of the Children's Ministry.
